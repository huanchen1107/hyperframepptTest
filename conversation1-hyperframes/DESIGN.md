## Style Prompt

Preserve the original clean academic presentation exactly, using restrained camera motion and soft crossfades so dense charts and Chinese text remain readable.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#161616`
- Slide paper: `#f4f2ed`
- Shadow: `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.24)`

## Typography

Typography is embedded in the rendered slide images and must not be replaced.

## Motion

- Five seconds per slide with a 0.6-second soft crossfade.
- Alternate subtle push-in and horizontal drift.
- Never crop, cover, or animate individual slide content.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not add captions, labels, or decorative overlays.
- Do not crop charts or text.
- Do not use energetic transitions or large camera moves.
- Do not recolor the original slides.
