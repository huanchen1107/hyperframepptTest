## Style Prompt

An animated Excalidraw-style strategy whiteboard. Every chart, system diagram,
card, arrow, and annotation is rebuilt with native HTML/CSS as if sketched live
by a market researcher. The canvas feels like warm paper, with imperfect dark
ink contours, pastel marker fills, handwritten labels, and energetic drawing
motions.

## Colors

- Paper canvas: `#fffdf6`
- Ink: `#1b1b1f`
- Blue marker: `#a8d8ff`
- Yellow marker: `#ffe9a8`
- Green marker: `#b9edcf`
- Red marker: `#ffc9c9`
- Muted ink: `#5f6068`

## Typography

- Headlines and labels: renderer-native `cursive`
- Data: `"Courier New", monospace`

## Motion

- Elements arrive like a presenter drawing them onto a whiteboard.
- Cards land with small alternating rotations and elastic easing.
- Arrows and progress bars draw from left to right.
- Scene transitions use broad pastel marker strokes.
- Captions remain clean, high-contrast, and synchronized to narration.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use screenshots of the original slides.
- Do not use glossy UI panels, neon glow, or dark control-room styling.
- Do not make every shape perfectly aligned or mechanically identical.
- Do not obscure captions or narration-driven visual beats.
